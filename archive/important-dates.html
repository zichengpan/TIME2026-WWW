Submission and Review Process

Paper Submission

The call for papers for TIME 2026 is now open. The submission deadline is December 18, 2025 (AoE). Authors should submit their papers through the OpenReview platform with all required information and supplementary materials.

For detailed submission requirements and author instructions, please refer to the Call for Papers page.

Review Process

All submissions undergo a double-blind peer review conducted by our Program Committee members. Reviews are conducted according to ACM and The Web Conference standards. Authors will be notified of acceptance decisions by January 13, 2026.

Camera-Ready Submission

Authors of accepted papers should submit their camera-ready versions by February 2, 2026. Accepted papers will be published in the WWW 2026 Companion Proceedings.

Workshop Event

The TIME 2026 Workshop will be held on April 13-14, 2026 in Dubai as part of The Web Conference 2026. At least one author from each accepted paper is expected to present their work at the workshop.
