Overview

The TIME 2026 workshop, organized as part of The Web Conference 2026 (WWW 2026), represents a pivotal initiative in advancing cross-domain knowledge exchange and methodological innovation in contemporary web technologies.

The initiative addresses critical digital ecosystem challenges including privacy, algorithmic bias, and ethical web practices. It serves as a collaboration platform for academics and industry experts focusing on social network analysis, graph algorithms, web mining, security, and ethics.

The workshop emphasizes actionable solutions and cross-disciplinary insights aligned with WWW 2026's objectives, bridging theoretical research and real-world applications.

Call for Papers

We invite researchers and practitioners to submit original, unpublished research papers addressing contemporary challenges in web technologies, responsible AI, and evaluation methodologies.

Submission Requirements

  • Format: ACM template (double-column), 4-8 pages (additional pages for references/appendices permitted)
  • Language: English
  • Review: Double-blind peer review by expert reviewers
  • Platform: OpenReview for submissions and reviews
  • Publication: Accepted papers published in WWW 2026 Companion Proceedings

Key Topics

  • Responsible and cross-domain AI evaluation frameworks
  • Trustworthy multimodal analytics and systems
  • Cross-domain benchmarking frameworks
  • Fairness, robustness, and transparency in AI
